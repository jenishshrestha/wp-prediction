Data corrections & verification notes

What we changed, and what we double-checked

Everything below was reconciled before the predictions were written.

  1. SoFIFA scrape came back clean this run. All four columns (OVR/ATT/MID/DEF) parsed correctly for every one of the 12 teams via the Playwright browser session — no zero/garbage defence values of the kind that have shown up in past pulls. Each rating was still sanity-checked against the FIFA-ranking order.
  2. Croatia's standing, corrected. Some live trackers listed Croatia on 0 pts / −2 GD. That's wrong: from the actual results (England 4–2 Croatia, Panama 0–1 Croatia) Croatia have 3 pts and −1 GD — confirmed by pre-match previews placing them "third on three points." We used the corrected figures, which also resolve the earlier −1 vs −3 goal-difference conflict in favour of −1.
  3. Ghana goalkeeper. First-choice Lawrence Ati Zigi remains injured; Benjamin Asare (who deputised in the 0–0 vs England) is expected to keep the gloves. Squad note, not a rating change.
  4. Live lineup-feed error (Algeria). A predicted-XI feed listed Luca Zidane as a striker and midfielder Maza in goal — reversed. Per verified team news, Zidane is Algeria's first-choice goalkeeper and starts there; the garbled outfield placement was ignored. Forward Mohamed Amoura misses out (hamstring).
  5. Confirmed facts re-verified: Mohammed Kudus is out (quad + hamstring relapse) and Carlos Queiroz is Ghana's manager; Luka Modrić is fit (recovered from a cheekbone fracture); Luca Zidane is Algeria's first-choice keeper and starts.
  6. Manager check: Croatia — Zlatko Dalić; Colombia — Néstor Lorenzo; England — Thomas Tuchel; Uzbekistan — Fabio Cannavaro.
  7. Ranking vs ratings divergence (flagged, not "fixed"): Ghana sit FIFA #73 yet carry a SoFIFA OVR of 75 — higher than DR Congo (#47) and Panama (#34). SoFIFA rewards Ghana's Premier-League-calibre individuals (Semenyo, the Williams/Ayew axis, Partey); FIFA's results-based ranking does not. We weighted both rather than trusting either alone.
