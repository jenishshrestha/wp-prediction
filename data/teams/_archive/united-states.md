# United States (USA) — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## Identity
- FIFA ranking: 17 (June 2026; dropped one spot ahead of the WC)
- Elo rating: ~1750–1800 (upper-mid tier; below the elite top-10)
- Squad market value: ~€350–400m (strong European-based core)
- Manager: Mauricio Pochettino (appointed 2024; attacking, high-intensity pedigree)
- Tactical identity: 4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1, possession + high press, attack through wide creators (Pulisic) and pace; co-host advantage with home crowds at every match.

## Qualification Path
Automatic qualification as co-host nation (USA/Mexico/Canada). No competitive qualifying; preparation via friendlies and the 2025 Gold Cup / Nations League cycle.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- 2022 World Cup: reached Round of 16 (lost to Netherlands). Young, energetic side.
- 2024 Copa América: group-stage exit, prompting coaching change to Pochettino.
- Core of the squad now in peak years (avg age ~27), 13 returnees from 2022.

## Stable Tendencies
- Attacking quality and pace on the flanks; can overwhelm lesser-organized opponents.
- Inconsistent: capable of big wins (5-1 Uruguay, 3-2 Senegal) and soft losses (2-5 Belgium, 0-2 Portugal, 1-2 Germany).
- Defensive lapses under sustained pressure; goalkeeping/CB depth a question.
- Significant lift from home crowds and familiarity as co-host.

## Live notes (as of 2026-06-13)
- Pre-WC form mixed: W3-2 Senegal (May 31), L1-2 Germany (June 6); 2 wins in last 5.
- Full-strength squad available for the opener; no major injuries/suspensions reported.
- Opening fixture played at SoFi Stadium (Inglewood) — strong home support, mild coastal climate, no altitude.
