# Switzerland — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## Identity
- FIFA ranking: ~19 (June 2026)
- Elo rating: ~1897 (Jan 2026 reading; ~13th in the world — solid top-20 side)
- Squad market value: ~€300m (Premier League / top-5-league core)
- Manager: Murat Yakin (since 2021)
- Tactical identity: Pragmatic 4-2-3-1 / 3-4-2-1; compact, disciplined defensive block, controlled possession, dangerous on transitions and set pieces. Xhaka dictates tempo from deep.

## Qualification Path
Won UEFA qualifying Group B: unbeaten, 14 pts from 6 (W4 D2), 14 GF / 2 GA. Home wins over Kosovo (4-0) and Slovenia (3-0), away 2-0 at Sweden, 0-0 in Slovenia, 4-1 v Sweden in Geneva, sealed top spot with 1-1 draw at Kosovo (18 Nov 2025). Excellent defensive record.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- Euro 2024: reached quarter-finals (eliminated by England on penalties after 1-1).
- 2022 World Cup: Round of 16 (lost 6-1 to Portugal).
- Consistent qualifier; reliable at reaching knockouts, rarely beaten by lower-ranked sides.
- Final warm-up: 1-1 draw with Australia (Ndoye scored early, Xhaka featured).

## Stable Tendencies
- Defensively very solid (only 2 conceded in qualifying); disciplined low-to-mid block.
- Score from a mix of set pieces, transitions, and wide overloads rather than sustained dominance.
- Tend to win narrowly and control games; not a high-scoring rout side but rarely loses to weaker opponents.
- No notable injuries/suspensions entering WC2026; full-strength squad.
