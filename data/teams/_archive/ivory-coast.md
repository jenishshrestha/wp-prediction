# Ivory Coast — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## Identity
- FIFA ranking: 33 (June 2026)
- Elo rating: ~1700 (approx; mid-tier, top African side)
- Squad market value: ~€350–380M (Amad €48.5M, O. Diomande €39.2M, Ndicka €36.7M; deep European-based talent)
- Manager: Emerse Faé (won AFCON 2023 as host after mid-tournament promotion)
- Tactical identity: 4-3-3, attack built around quick, direct wingers; energetic midfield; can be vulnerable defensively in transition

## Qualification Path
Qualified through CAF qualifying as a strong group winner. Reigning AFCON champions (2023, won on home soil under Faé after a dramatic group-stage recovery).

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- AFCON 2023 (host): **Champions** — survived a chaotic group stage, sacked coach mid-tournament, Faé led the turnaround to the title. Resilient, big-game mentality.
- Strong recent form: four-match winning streak into the World Cup; five wins in last six, including a **2-1 friendly win over France** the week before the tournament.

## Stable Tendencies
- Goals-for: capable of scoring through wide overloads and individual quality; not prolific but varied.
- Goals-against: the relative weakness — can be opened up in transition, and the absence of Ndicka thins the centre-back depth.
- Set pieces: physical, decent aerial threat.
- Form line (last 6, all comps): W-W-W-W-L-W.
