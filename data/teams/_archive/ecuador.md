# Ecuador — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## Identity
- FIFA ranking: 23 (June 2026)
- Elo rating: ~1820 (approx; upper-mid tier, second-best CONMEBOL qualifier behind Argentina)
- Squad market value: ~€500M+ (elite spine: Caicedo, Pacho, Hincapié, Estupiñán all at top European clubs)
- Manager: Sebastián Beccacece (Argentine; aim "defend like Arsenal, attack like Barcelona")
- Tactical identity: Pragmatic, defensively elite; sits compact, absorbs pressure, counters through pace (Plata, Angulo). Low-block discipline; controlled build-up via Caicedo.

## Qualification Path
CONMEBOL qualifying: finished **2nd, behind only Argentina**. Conceded just **5 goals in 18 qualifiers** (best defense), scored only 14 — won 29 points. (Began the campaign with a points deduction and still finished 2nd — exceptional.)

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- Copa América 2024: competitive, reached knockouts.
- WC 2022: eliminated in group stage but defensively organised.
- "Golden Generation" label; widely tipped as a 2026 sleeper/dark horse.

## Stable Tendencies
- Goals-against: **elite** — 19-match unbeaten run with 13 clean sheets; last defeat Sept 2024 vs Brazil. Among the best defenses in the tournament.
- Goals-for: **low/efficient** — only ~14–20 goals across the unbeaten run; relies on counters, set pieces, individual moments. Recent 3-0 friendly win over Guatemala an outlier.
- Recent friendlies: 1-1 USA, 1-1 Mexico, 0-0 Canada, 2-0 New Zealand, 3-0 Guatemala.
- Form line (last 6, all comps): W-W-D-D-W-D — hard to beat, often low-scoring.
