## Qualification Path
Won African qualifying **Group G** (sealed with a 3–0 win over Somalia). Returning to the World Cup for the first time since **2014** — regarded as one of Africa's most dangerous sides and a live upset threat.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- No World Cup since 2014 (round of 16 that year, famously taking Germany to extra time).
- AFCON: group-stage disappointments in 2022/2024 after winning the title in 2019.
- Pattern: technically gifted and capable of beating anyone on their day; historically inconsistent and prone to flat tournament outings.

## Stable Tendencies
- High attacking ceiling driven by Mahrez/Amoura quality and pace on the break.
- Currently in outstanding form — strong defensively and ruthless going forward in warm-ups.
- Boom-or-bust temperament: can produce a marquee result or an underwhelming display.
