# Morocco (Atlas Lions) — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## Identity
- FIFA ranking: ~7 (June 2026) — highest in their history
- Elo rating: ~1820–1870 (strong upper-mid tier)
- Squad market value: ~€450–550m (European-based core: PSG, Real Madrid, Premier League)
- Manager: Mohamed Ouahbi (appointed March 2026 after Walid Regragui departed; emphasizes tactical control/organization)
- Tactical identity: Disciplined, compact defensive block; 4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1 with quick transitions and dangerous wide/fullback overlaps. Defense-first identity that frustrates elite opponents (cf. 2022 run).

## Qualification Path
Cruised through CAF qualifying (e.g. 2-0 over Zambia), one of Africa's most convincing campaigns. Reigning AFCON 2025 finalists.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- 2022 World Cup: **4th place** — beat Belgium, Spain, Portugal; first African/Arab semi-finalist. Built on defensive resilience + transitions.
- AFCON 2025: reached the final; Brahim Díaz top scorer.
- Pattern: elite organization, low goals-against, comfortable defending deep against favorites and striking on the break / from fullbacks.

## Stable Tendencies
- Strong defensive solidity and shape; concede few clear chances against superior sides.
- Attacking threat from Hakimi overlaps, Brahim Díaz creativity, and set pieces rather than a fixed elite striker.
- Can struggle to break down deep blocks themselves; output dipped without a recognized No.9 (En-Nesyri not selected).
