# Haiti (Les Grenadiers) — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## Identity
- FIFA ranking: ~83 (June 2026)
- Elo rating: ~1500–1550 (lower-mid tier; clearly the group's weakest)
- Squad market value: ~€40–60m (modest; a few European-based players, mostly lower/mid-tier clubs + diaspora)

- Tactical identity: 4-4-2 / 4-2-3-1; sit deep, stay compact, look to spring quick forwards on the break. Limited possession control vs better sides; reliant on transition and individual moments.

## Qualification Path
Won the Third Round of CONCACAF qualifying (finished first in their group) to earn **direct qualification** — first World Cup since 1974 (52-year wait). Nazon was instrumental (6 goals, hat-trick vs Costa Rica).

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- No World Cup appearances since 1974; minimal recent major-tournament pedigree.
- Gold Cup participation in CONCACAF; generally a step below the region's top tier (USA/Mexico/Canada/Costa Rica) but competitive.
- Pattern: organized, hard-working underdog; capable of strong counter-attacking results (e.g. 4-0 over New Zealand) but inconsistent against quality.

## Stable Tendencies
- Defensive resilience variable; can be opened up by sustained pressure and quality movement.
- Attack depends on Isidor/Nazon finishing transitions and Bellegarde's creativity; struggles to break down organized defenses.
- Young squad (avg age ~24, only five players over 30) — energy and athleticism, but tournament inexperience.
