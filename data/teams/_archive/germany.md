## Qualification Path
Won UEFA Group A: 5 wins, 0 draws, 1 loss; 16 goals for, 3 against. Qualified directly as group winners.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
Quarter-finalists / strong showings as host of Euro 2024; long pedigree (4-time world champions). Among the favourites/contenders for WC 2026 despite patchy recent form.

## Stable Tendencies
High-scoring attacking unit (16 goals in 6 qualifiers). Tends to dominate possession heavily vs weaker sides and pile on goals once a game opens. Defensive solidity occasionally questioned against elite opposition but rarely troubled by minnows. 26-man squad named 21 May 2026 (Bayern spine + Wirtz). No injuries or suspensions reported entering the tournament; Curaçao on 14 June is Germany's opener (no group game played yet as of 13 June). Musiala available — fitness is the only lingering watch but Nagelsmann has downplayed concern.
