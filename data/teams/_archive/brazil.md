## Qualification Path
Qualified via CONMEBOL but endured an unusually difficult campaign, finishing 5th — well off their usual standard, with several poor performances and away defeats. Ancelotti took over mid-cycle to stabilize.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- 2022 World Cup: quarter-final exit (lost on penalties to Croatia) despite being among favorites.
- 2024 Copa América: quarter-final exit.
- Pattern: abundant attacking talent, recurring questions over midfield control and big-game composure.

## Stable Tendencies
- High goals-for ceiling driven by individual wide forwards (Vini, Raphinha); can score in bursts (6-2 Panama, 5-0 South Korea in 2026 warm-ups).
- Generally solid defensively with Alisson + Marquinhos, but can be exposed in transition when fullbacks push high.
- Creativity historically concentrated in a few stars; thinner when key creators are absent.
