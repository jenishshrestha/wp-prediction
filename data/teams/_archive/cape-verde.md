# Cape Verde (Cabo Verde) — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

- FIFA ranking: ~67
- Elo rating: ~1600 (low-to-mid tier internationally)
- Squad market value: ~€49.25 million (one of the lowest at WC 2026; only one

  and direct on transitions; relies on collective discipline over individual star

## Qualification Path
**Historic first-ever World Cup qualification.** Topped their CAF qualifying group
ahead of Mohamed Salah's Egypt — the standout achievement of the cycle and a major
upset given resources. Second-smallest nation (by population) ever to reach a
men's World Cup.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- No prior World Cup appearances (2026 is the debut).
- AFCON regulars; reached AFCON quarter-finals (2013, 2023) — punch above weight
  but limited experience at the very top level.

## Stable Tendencies
- Defensively organised and hard to break down; concede modestly when compact.
- Low chance-creation volume against elite sides; goals come mainly from
  transitions, set pieces, and Mendes/Livramento moments.
- Limited squad depth and few players accustomed to facing top-tier opposition
  week in, week out.
