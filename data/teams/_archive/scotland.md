## Qualification Path
Qualified for first World Cup since 1998 (ended 28-year absence; ninth WC appearance overall). Reached via European qualifying under Steve Clarke.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- Euro 2020 and Euro 2024: group-stage exits (never advanced from a major-tournament group).
- 2026 is their first World Cup since France 1998; still chasing a first-ever knockout-stage qualification at a World Cup.
- Pattern: competitive, organized, can frustrate stronger sides; goals can be hard to come by against deep blocks.

## Stable Tendencies
- Defensive organization and work rate are the foundation; set pieces a genuine weapon (Robertson delivery, Souttar/CBs aerial).
- Goals shared across midfield runners (McTominay, McGinn) rather than a prolific No.9.
- Tournament record of underperforming expectations — mental hurdle in opening games.
