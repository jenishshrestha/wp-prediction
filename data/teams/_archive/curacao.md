# Curaçao — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## Identity
- FIFA ranking: ~82 (April 2026)
- Elo rating: roughly ~1500s tier (Caribbean mid-tier)
- Squad market value: very low relative to elite nations (Dutch lower-league / MLS / Eredivisie-fringe based)
- Manager: Dick Advocaat (78 — oldest head coach in WC history; first to lead three different nations at a WC)
- Tactical identity: Pragmatic, compact defensive block, sit deep vs stronger sides and look to counter / set pieces; more proactive vs Concacaf peers.

## Qualification Path
Topped Concacaf qualifying group containing Jamaica, Trinidad & Tobago, Bermuda. Unbeaten through qualifying; sealed it with a 0-0 draw vs Jamaica. Scored the most goals (28) and led xG (22.7) in Concacaf qualifying — but against Concacaf-level opposition. Smallest nation by area (444 km²) and population (<160,000) ever to reach a World Cup.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
World Cup debutant. No prior major-tournament finals experience at this level.

## Stable Tendencies
Effective in attack vs Concacaf peers, but big quality gap vs elite sides shows in recent friendlies: lost 2-0 to China, 5-1 to Australia, and 4-1 to Scotland in Glasgow (Advocaat's first game back, 30 May). Final send-off was a 4-0 win over Aruba in Willemstad — a low-quality opponent that flatters the attack. 26-man squad confirmed under Advocaat (who returned after stepping down early 2026); no injuries or suspensions reported. Concedes heavily against well-organised, higher-quality opponents; defensively vulnerable in transition and under sustained pressure. Carries an attacking threat (Chong) but very limited expected output vs a top side.
