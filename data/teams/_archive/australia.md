## Qualification Path
Qualified via AFC route (third round group winners/runners-up), reaching their sixth straight World Cup. Solid, defense-first qualifying campaign; not prolific but hard to beat.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- 2022 World Cup: reached Round of 16 (beat Tunisia & Denmark, lost 1-2 to Argentina) — a resilient, organized campaign.
- 2023 Asian Cup: quarter-finals.
- Identity: low-event, hard-working sides that grind out narrow results and rely on set pieces and transitions.

## Stable Tendencies
- Defensively stable and physical under Popovic; concede few clear chances when set.
- Low goals-for output; struggle to break down organized opponents — rely on set pieces (Souttar) and counters.
- Strong in the air both boxes; can frustrate technically superior teams.
- Inconsistent recent form (won ~2 of last 5); big home win 5-1 Curaçao (Mar 31) the high point.
