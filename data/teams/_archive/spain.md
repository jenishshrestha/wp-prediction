# Spain — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

- FIFA ranking: 1 (top of the world rankings as of June 2026)
- Elo rating: ~2120 (elite tier; one of the top 2–3 sides by Elo)

## Qualification Path
Qualified comfortably as a top European side; reigning Euro 2024 champions and
UEFA Nations League finalists 2025 (lost final to Portugal on penalties). Entered
WC 2026 as one of the leading title favourites.

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- Euro 2024: Champions (beat England in final via Oyarzabal winner).
- Nations League 2025: Runners-up (lost final to Portugal on penalties after a
  9-goal thriller win vs France in the semis).
- Unbeaten in normal time for ~28 games since March 2024.

## Stable Tendencies
- High goals-for through sustained possession and chance volume; can score in
  bursts against deep blocks but sometimes labours to break down packed defences
  early.
- Very solid defensively; concedes little, dominates territory.
- Strong from open play and increasingly from set pieces; full-strength spine
  (Rodri, Pedri, Cubarsí, Laporte) intact for the opener even with the two star
  wingers rested.
