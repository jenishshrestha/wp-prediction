## Qualification Path
Qualified via UEFA play-offs (March 2026), beating Romania and Kosovo in the play-off route. Lost only once in qualifying (to Spain). First World Cup since 2002 (when they finished 3rd).

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- EURO 2024: reached quarter-finals (beat Austria in R16; lost narrowly to Netherlands) — exciting, attacking, technically gifted side.
- 2002 World Cup: 3rd place — their last WC appearance before 2026.
- Identity: technically strong, creative through young stars; capable of dominating possession but occasionally vulnerable defensively.

## Stable Tendencies
- Strong attacking output and chance creation via Güler/Yıldız/Çalhanoğlu; set-piece danger.
- Good goals-for record; can break down compact defenses with individual quality.
- Defensive lapses under pressure / on transitions — can concede against organized counter-attacking sides.
- Won 7 of last 10 (since Mar 2025), including a 2-1 win over the USA.
