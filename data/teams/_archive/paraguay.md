# Paraguay (La Albirroja) — Base Profile

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

## Identity
- FIFA ranking: ~40 (June 2026; some sources ~48)
- Elo rating: ~1650–1700 (mid tier)
- Squad market value: ~€120–150m (Brazilian Série A core + a few European/MLS players)
- Manager: Gustavo Alfaro (Argentinian; appointed Aug 2024, transformed the side)
- Tactical identity: Disciplined 4-4-2 — compact defensive block, high work-rate, physicality, transition/set-piece threat rather than sustained possession.

## Qualification Path
Qualified via CONMEBOL, finishing 6th (last automatic spot) with 28 points over 18 games. Form team of the second half of qualifying under Alfaro — home wins over Argentina and Brazil. Ends a 16-year World Cup absence (their longest ever).

## Recent Major-Tournament History
- Last World Cup appearance was 2010 (reached quarter-finals). 16-year gap since.
- 2024 Copa América: group-stage exit (pre-Alfaro), prompting the managerial change.
- Track record at major tournaments historically built on defensive resilience.

## Stable Tendencies
- Defense-first identity: only 14 scored / 10 conceded across 18 qualifiers — low-scoring, tight games.
- Limited attacking output; reliant on Almirón/Enciso individual quality and set pieces.
- Physical, organized, hard to break down when compact; vulnerable when forced to chase a game or stretched by pace.

## Live notes (as of 2026-06-13)
- Opened Group D vs USA at SoFi Stadium (away-equivalent, hostile crowd, no altitude — neutralizes any South American conditioning edge).
- Full squad named June 1; Gómez captaining. No major reported absences for the opener.
- Adjusting up from CONMEBOL grind to host-nation atmosphere.
